This whitepaper documents the QNeX Codec (Quantized Nonlinear eXchange), a lossy, human-centric image compression architecture designed as a modern alternative to JPEG. It details the algorithmic progression from basic spatial linear algebra (SVD) to a content-adaptive, Dual-Domain Fusion model.
