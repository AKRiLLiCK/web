This whitepaper documents the Sigmera Codec (Open Linear Algebra Codec), a lossy, human-centric image compression architecture designed as a modern alternative to JPEG. It details the algorithmic progression from basic spatial linear algebra (SVD) to a content-adaptive, Dual-Domain Fusion model.
